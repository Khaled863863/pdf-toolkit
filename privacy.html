Last updated: 2026-04-15

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information PDF Toolkit ("we", "us", "our") collects when you use our website (the "Service"), and how it is used. By using the Service, you agree to the terms described here.

2. Your Files Stay on Your Device

All PDF processing tools on this website run entirely within your browser. When you select or drop a file into one of our tools, that file is read into your browser's memory and processed locally. We do not upload your files to any server, and we do not store them.

3. Information We Collect Automatically

Like most websites, our hosting provider may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamp) for security and diagnostic purposes. We also use aggregate analytics to understand which tools are popular, but we do not attempt to identify individual users.

4. Cookies

We may use first-party cookies or local storage to remember your preferences (e.g., interface theme). Third-party services like Google AdSense and analytics providers may set their own cookies as described below.

This site is supported by advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.

  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Learn more about how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

6. Third-Party Libraries (CDNs)

To power our tools we load open-source JavaScript libraries (such as PDF-Lib and PDF.js) from public content delivery networks. Your browser makes a network request to these CDNs to download the library code itself — this request does not include your files.

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
