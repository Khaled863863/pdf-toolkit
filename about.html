About PDF Toolkit

PDF Toolkit is a free online suite of tools that helps you work with PDF files without installing any software. Everything runs directly in your web browser using modern JavaScript libraries like PDF-Lib and PDF.js, which means your files stay on your computer and are never uploaded to our servers.

Our Mission

We believe basic PDF operations should be free, fast, and private. Too many online tools ask you to sign up, watch ads before processing, or even upload sensitive documents to unknown servers. PDF Toolkit takes a different approach: the whole application is a static website, and the heavy lifting happens inside your browser tab.

How It Works

When you open a tool like "Merge PDF" or "Compress PDF," the required JavaScript libraries load from a public CDN. The moment you drop a file, it is read into your browser's memory, processed locally, and offered back to you as a downloadable file. No network requests carry your document.

What Makes Us Different

  • Privacy-first: your files never leave your device.
  • Always free: no subscription tiers, no "pro" unlock gates.
  • No sign-up: jump straight into the tool you need.
  • Works offline: once a tool page loads, it can keep working without internet.

How We Keep It Free

Running a free service still costs something — hosting, domain fees, and development time. To cover these costs, this website displays non-intrusive advertisements served through Google AdSense. Ads help us keep all tools 100% free to use, forever. We do not show paywalls, we do not limit file sizes artificially, and we do not sell user data.
